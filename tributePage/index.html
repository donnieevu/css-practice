Monkey D. Luffy

King of Pirates

Here's a detailed list of his crew members:

  • Roronoa Zoro: a combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a swordsmen that wields 3 swords.
  • Nami: the navigator for the ship. Knowledgable in reading maps and guiding the crew on their journey in the ocean.
  • Usopp: The sniper of the team. Uses a slingshot as his primary weapon.
  • Sanji: The cook of the ship. He is infatuated with women and uses only his legs for battle.
  • Tony Tony Chopper: The doctor of the ship. He is a reindeer who ate a special fruit that allows himself to transform into a human.
  • Nico Robin: The archaeologist of the group. She analyzes fossils that leads to her goal, finding the true history of the world.
  • Franky: The shipwright of the crew. He repairs the ship and is a cyborg.
